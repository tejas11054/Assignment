🍝 Creamy Alfredo Pasta

A simple and delicious recipe for pasta lovers!

🛒 Ingredients

🍝
200g Fettuccine Pasta
🧈
2 tbsp Butter
🧄
3 Garlic cloves (minced)
🥛
1 cup Fresh cream
🧀
½ cup Grated Parmesan
🧂
Salt & Pepper (to taste)
🌿
Fresh parsley (for garnish)

👩‍🍳 Cooking Steps

Boil the pasta in salted water until al dente.

Sauté garlic in butter, then add cream and parmesan.

Mix pasta with sauce, season, and garnish with parsley.
