🍝 Creamy Alfredo Pasta

A simple and delicious recipe for pasta lovers!

🛒 Ingredients

  • 200g Fettuccine pasta
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 3 Garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 cup Fresh cream
  • ½ cup Grated parmesan
  • Salt & Pepper (to taste)
  • Fresh parsley (for garnish)
